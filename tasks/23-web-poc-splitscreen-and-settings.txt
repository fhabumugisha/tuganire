Thymeleaf, HTMX 2, Alpine 3, Tailwind 4. Alpine state toggles between "deux boutons" and "split-screen" modes (US-04) without a page reload. Settings: a provider <select> populated from GET /api/v1/providers, persisting the choice via PUT /api/v1/providers/tts (hx:post/hx:put with CSRF). Onboarding: 3 lightweight screens, skippable (PRD UX rule "pas de tutoriel obligatoire"). Keep design tokens in static/css/input.css @theme.
